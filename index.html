Hey I'm App Name.


Get a free sentiment check on your messages. App Name is trained to detect the aggressiveness of a sentence of text. Each sentence is graded based on the choice of words used with a helpful comment that informs the user of a possible perception of hostility from the statement.

How does App Name work?


App Name was created using a Natural Language API in the Google Cloud Platform. This API provides powerful machine learning models related to Sentiment Analysis, that was filtered to allow interpretation of text in relation to aggression. This interpretation was then formatted into a user friendly structure for the user.







Why App Name?


App Name was created to address the growing dependence of society on digital forms of communication. Today's youth spends an average of 9 hours everyday consuming and utilizing online media per day. As a result, a large portion of such an individual's development will be based on their experiences and interations using text. App Name hopes to bring an awareness of the possible implications and consequences of an individual's words on other members of society.
