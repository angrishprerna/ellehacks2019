Hey, I'm Friendlier.


Get a free sentiment check on your messages. Friendlier is trained to detect the sentiment of a sentence of text. The input text is analysed for aggresion level and application responds with helpful comment that informs the user of a possible perception of hostility from the statement.

How does Friendlier work?


Friendlier was created using a Natural Language API in the Google Cloud Platform. This API provides how aggressive level of the text by provding us with a score ranging -1 to +1 with negative value indicating aggression of the text and a positive score indicating a non-aggressive text. This interpretation was then formatted into an easy to understand structure for our users.







Why Friendlier?


Friendlier was created to address the growing dependence of society on digital forms of communication. Today's youth spends an average of 9 hours everyday consuming and utilizing online media per day. As a result, a large portion of such an individual's development will be based on their experiences and interations using text. Friendlier hopes to bring an awareness of the possible implications and consequences of an individual's words on other members of society.
